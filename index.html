About Me

Background, values, and what I bring to a team

I'm a Senior Software Engineer with over 8 years of experience designing and shipping production systems at scale. My career spans three high-stakes industries — Fintech (Fanatics), E-commerce (Walmart), and Healthcare (E-POT) — giving me a broad lens on building reliable, user-facing software.

I thrive on problems that sit at the intersection of real-time data, complex business logic, and great developer experience. Whether it's a Kafka pipeline processing thousands of events per second or a React dashboard that replaces hours of manual work, I focus on solutions that create measurable impact.

My debugging philosophy: reproduce first, isolate the layer, fix the root cause — not the symptom. I write tests to prevent regressions, not just to hit coverage numbers.

Outside engineering, I mentor junior developers and collaborate closely with product and design to ship features end-to-end — from stakeholder requirements through to post-launch optimization.
